Who We Are

Celtec is an independant game development studio driven by creativity and innovation. Founded in
2002, we've been dedicated to crating unique, player-focused games that push boudnaries and spark
imagination.



What We Do

At Celtec Games, we create unique video game experiences for all major platforms, which includes
consoles, PC and mobile that captivate and challenge. Whether it's shooters or horror, we experiment
with new gameplay mechanics and bring life into out games.



Our Mission

Our mission at Celtec is to craft games that inspire, connect and entertain. we believe in building
more than just games, we're building experiences that resonate, stories that stick and communities
that thrive. Through bold ideas and thoughtful design, we strive to push the industry forward one
game at a time.
